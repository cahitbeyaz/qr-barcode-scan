Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 24 November 2025

No Data Collection

The application QR & Barcode Scan does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from users.

Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect or process:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any data entered or scanned by the user

All QR and barcode scanning, as well as QR code generation, occur locally on the device. No scanned content or generated data is uploaded or sent to any server.

Camera and Photos Access

The app uses the device camera only for scanning QR codes and barcodes. The app may access the photo library only when the user chooses to scan a QR code from an image. These permissions are used solely for their intended purposes and no data is collected or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

The iOS version of this app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or external SDKs.

Data Sharing

Since the app does not collect any data, it does not share any data with any third parties.
